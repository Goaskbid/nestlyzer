# Regulatory and disclosure

Operator: Torsten Sauter, Alter Landstr. 77, 8706 Meilen, Switzerland. Contact: torsten.sauter@icloud.com.

Nestlyzer is a research and decision-support website. It is not a public authority, financial adviser, tax adviser, legal adviser, real-estate broker, medical adviser or immigration adviser.

Rankings are not recommendations to buy, sell, move, invest, retire, immigrate, choose a school or choose healthcare. Verify all material decisions with responsible authorities or qualified advisers.

Advertising is labelled and separated from rankings, scoring inputs and editorial place context.

The static app has no account system. Guided-profile answers and filters run in the browser. Public enrichment calls can be made to official datasets, maps, Wikimedia, Wikidata or OpenStreetMap services. Those services may receive normal technical request data.

Before commercial launch, obtain legal review for Swiss FADP, EU GDPR, advertising, cookie, analytics, consumer disclosure and platform obligations in target markets.
